WHAT IS RENEWABLE ENERGY?

Renewable energy, aslo known as green energy the foundation of green power that is
generated from natural sources which are constantly replenished and do not run out.

Green power is an initiative dedicated to promoting renewable energy;

SOURCES OF RENEWABLE ENERGY

Solar energy

Harnessing energy from the sun's rays using photovoltaic panels or solaer thermal systems to generate electricity or heat.

Biogas

Producing energy from organic matter, such as wood,crops or waste through combustion, anaerobic digestion or conversion to biofuels.
